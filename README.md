# IY2840-Network-Attacks
---

**Coursework Summary: Understanding Attacks and Mitigations**

This coursework delves into the critical aspects of cybersecurity, focusing on various types of attacks and effective mitigation strategies. Through comprehensive review of lecture materials, course texts, and extensive research from online and library resources, the coursework provides insights into:

- **Types of Cyber Attacks**: Explore common cyber threats such as phishing, malware, DDoS attacks, and social engineering.
- **Attack Vectors**: Understand how these attacks exploit vulnerabilities in systems and networks.
- **Mitigation Techniques**: Learn effective measures to protect against cyber threats, including encryption, intrusion detection/prevention systems (IDS/IPS), secure coding practices, and user awareness training.
- **Case Studies and Examples**: Analyze real-world incidents and their impact, emphasizing the importance of proactive cybersecurity measures.

The coursework encourages concise and succinct responses, utilizing bullet points and sketches where applicable to present findings effectively. Submissions are expected to uphold academic integrity, with proper acknowledgment of all utilized resources and adherence to referencing guidelines.
